About Us

RoundSharp Solutions is a hardware specific brand established in the early 1970s. We are the best in the business and provide all kinds of solutions and machines for cutting and trimming. Our owner Mr. Bernard Laste is the prime example of how leader conducts his company. Customer satisfaction is our motto and utmost cared about in our company! Teamwork, Resiliency, excellent customer service is our utmost priority!
